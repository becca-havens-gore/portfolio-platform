About Me

Designing coherent product systems for technical teams at scale

I'm a Principal Product Designer specializing in developer-adjacent platforms, security workflows, and scalable design systems. For over a decade I've designed products for security engineers, SOC analysts, IT practitioners, and platform teams — users who think in systems, work across fragmented tools, and need fast paths from signal to investigation to action.

My work spans four design systems across Rapid7, AlphaWave, and Kronologic, where I've led MUI migrations, built visualization frameworks, established governance models, and pioneered AI experience standards. I believe great design systems are more than component libraries — they're shared languages that unite design and engineering around a common vision.

When I'm not designing platform experiences, I'm mentoring designers, building Figma plugins, or experimenting with AI tooling. I bring deep technical empathy, systems architecture thinking, and a bias toward measurable clarity into every problem I work on.
